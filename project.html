Santa Cruz x Creatures of the Night

Combining the subculture genres of skate and old horror films to create Santa Cruise Skateboads occult, eclectic collection.

Santa Cruz Skateboards are known for their outlandish designs and incorporated bright, vibrant colors. Inspired by old movie monster films, Santa Cruz is releasing a black and white special collection to commerate these films. Through this revamp, we will target a broader audience that will intruige not just the youger generation of horror movie fans, but older fans as well. What sets this collection apart is the strength in the designs and concepts shown through the lack of color and use of grayscale. This collection pays tribute to these old films and appeals to horror fans everywhere.
